Today, there are only about 2,500 red pandas left on this planet. Rapid human population growth in the Eastern Himalayas is causing deforestation and fragmentation of the red panda habitat.  If this continues, we will not just lose the only living members of the A. Fulgens taxonomic family, but will endanger clouded leopards, Himalayan black bears, and hundreds of birds species under the red panda umbrella ecosystem.

Each dollar you share helps grow the Red Panda Community forest by funding:
• The Anti-Poaching Network
• Forest Conservation Nurseries
• Forest Fire Fighting Teams
• Sustainable Herding

OUR FOUNDERS

In 2019, we found out about the sad reality of vanishing ecosystems for the endangered Red Panda. Instead of looking the other way, we saw an opportunity to make a difference by creating SaveRed and donating proceeds to preserving the Red Panda Community Forest.

We have always believed the future of endangered species and humanity are intrinsically linked, and, since then, have worked tirelessly with top brands in delivering fashion with a purpose. Today, with over a million followers, dozens of collaborations, and 55,000 acres of Red Panda forest conserved, we’d just like to say…

Thank you.

-David, Flo & Jae
